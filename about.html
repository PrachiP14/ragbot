About Our Chatbot

The College Administrative Chatbot is designed to provide instant answers to your questions about college policies, procedures, and administrative matters.

Our Mission

Our mission is to streamline the administrative process for students, faculty, and staff by providing instant access to information through an intelligent chatbot. We aim to reduce the time spent searching for answers and improve the overall experience of interacting with college administration.

How It Works

The chatbot uses advanced natural language processing and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to understand your questions and provide accurate answers based on college documentation. It continuously learns from interactions to improve its responses over time.
